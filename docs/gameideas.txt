        Visuals:

        - Leather/chains/paint.
    
    - The Rustborn - Icon B
        Origin: 
            - They are the robots / AI that remained after The Struggle.

    - The Zenith - Icon Z

            - They original elites human from the Old War that created the robots.

        Visuals:
        - Smoother panels
        - Electric engines
        - Batteries/electronics however with a focus on solar power.
