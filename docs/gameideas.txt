- The Struggle:
    Was some major war that lead to the creation of the 3 factions, well in the past. 

- Factions:
    - The Remnant
        Origin:
            - They are the humans that remained after The Struggle.

        Visuals:
        - Rusted welded steel
        - Petrol engines
        - Leather/chains/paint.
    
    - The Rustborn
        Origin: 
            - They are the robots / AI that remained after The Struggle.
            - They were created by The Zenith orignally.

        Visuals:
        - Salvaged plating, prefering rivots
        - Petrol and electric engines
        - Batteries/electronics

    - The Zenith
        Origin:
            - They original elites from the Old War that created the robots.

        Visuals:
        - Smoother panels
        - Electric engines
        - Batteries/electronics however with a focus on solar power.



- Vehicles
    - We are going have a couple base chassis then a whole ton of parts/attachments.
    - The idea then is each faction will favor certain parts and colors.
    - Each faction will have their own special parts.
